# the exterminator came back. and then everything got worse. okay so. you know how i said some mysteries don't get solved? yeah i lied. some of them got solved tonight. in the worst possible way. so it started simple enough. i smelled Raid. i smelled blood. the lights were off. and then i heard it. the exterminator said, in that same ominous voice, that he had to exterminate the pests. he was back. same cloak. same mask. same line. same everything. i went to check on Bree and found her hiding under her bed, barely able to get the words out. she told me, in her tiny little voice, that the big scary guy had tried to spray her. she is five years old and one inch tall and this man looked at her and saw a pest. so i grabbed her, put her in my pocket, grabbed the gun, and went to find him. i shot him. many times. he kept talking. something that sounded like robot gibberish — and then i realized. that voice. i knew that voice. it was Bree's voice. the gaymova_ODD.wav. the little robot scream from when i was messing around with the marimba in Scratch. it was always Bree. she was the cursed audio this whole time and none of us knew. eventually he went down. i thought it was over. i got Bree out of my pocket and she was unconscious. suffocated. i forgot she can't breathe in a pocket because she is one inch tall and i am not always thinking clearly when there is a cloaked man in my house. i put her on my desk and opened Scratch to do something, anything. Scratch was under maintenance. for everyone else on earth it was fine. just me. then the doorbell rang. it was Niles. bedhead as always, scar on his forehead, holding M&Ms because it was Hanukkah. he asked if Bree was around. i grabbed him, closed the door, and just showed him what was on the desk. he stepped off my hand, got down to her level, and held up a piece of paper. he told her to tap the desk twice if she could hear him. nothing. he said it again. and then something happened. she tapped. Niles looked up at me and said that this was my fault, wasn't it. i explained everything. the exterminator. the Raid. the pest comment. i told him i had to kill him. Niles said oh, and then i mean, and then went back to focusing on Bree. i needed her conscious enough to finally decode the cipher from my wiped Scratch project. i said the magic words — milkshake for lunch kingdom — and she drew the entire cipher alphabet without hesitation. 26 dollar bill variations. one for each letter. i sat down at my computer and decoded the message that had been left behind in the wreckage of my Scratch project before it disappeared. it said: EXT. IS REAS. the exterminator is reasonable. someone had been trying to warn me the whole time. and the message was signed by someone named Marlene. Niles went very quiet. Marlene is his mom. we didn't have long to process that because Marlene chose that exact moment to burst through my unlocked front door, grab Niles, and drag him out into the backyard. i watched through the window. she told him he was a dumbf**k. he told her his name was Niles. she told him that was according to his father apparently. and then she used her giant hands and ripped his head clean off. i stood at the window with Bree on my shoulder and didn't say anything. and then from behind me, from the floor where i had left him for dead, the exterminator spoke. he said he had successfully exterminated one pest. he was keeping score. Niles was pest number one. Bree was pest number two — still alive, so she didn't count yet. Marlene had sent him after both of them. i shot him again. many more times than before. then i threw him outside. Marlene reached through the window and stole my notepad. just grabbed it and launched it across the street like it was nothing. this notepad is where i write everything for the blog. she wanted the story gone. i went and retrieved it. then i walked to Niles' dad's house. his actual dad. not the exterminator. i'm sleeping here tonight. Bree is with me, holding the notepad. Marlene is still out there. the exterminator is dead. again. probably for real this time. Niles is gone. and i still don't know why Marlene wanted them both exterminated, or how she got into my Scratch files, or what the rest of the cipher said, or why TurboWarp is still darker with no changelog. some mysteries get solved. and then new ones show up immediately to replace them.
