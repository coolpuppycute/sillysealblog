why i think my sister is up to something, or something else


okay so today was a DAY. so i was working on my scratch game, Sneak Out, right? i needed a game over theme so i went to
Ultrabox to compose one. it actually turned out pretty good! ...except when i exported it, the audio was so quiet it was basically nonexistent. like, completely inaudible. flat line on the waveform. nothing. BUT. it came with a free crystal marimba sound effect bundled in for some reason. no explanation. just here's a random marimba, enjoy.
(Audio file goes here!) so naturally i started messing around with it in Scratch because i do that when i'm bored. i edit audio files for fun. and then... something happened. i exported it as a wav and when i played it back it sounded like a tiny little robotic voice. very obscure. very unhinged. and what it said — or at least what i could make out — was: "WAAAA... don't kill... MEE!!!" the first and last parts were high pitched and extremely loud. the middle was calmer. it was either "don't kill me" or some variation involving donuts and stools. i still don't know. i named the file gaymova_ODD.wav because gaymova is literally what the game over sprite is called in my project file and the whole thing was just deeply odd. then i switched over to TurboWarp because i needed an extension that's exclusive to it. and i noticed something. the UI — which is normally red — was a darker shade. like, noticeably darker. almost blood colored. i went looking for a changelog. a devlog. any mention of it anywhere online. nothing. no one talked about it. it just... changed. silently. then Sneak Out got wiped. the whole project. gone. except for one single sprite that was left behind. it had text on it — but not normal text. it was written in some kind of cipher, using a font i had never seen before. the characters looked like dollar signs and... okay this is hard to explain but some of them looked like butt cheeks. some looked like people using a PC. some looked like people sitting on a couch. i tried to change the font to figure out what it said. the font wasn't in the dropdown. i tried to edit the text directly. it was locked. completely uneditable. i tried to at least map out an alphabet so i could decode it manually. and then it disappeared too. i have no idea what it said. around this time i also found a PNG file sitting in my System32 folder. at first i panicked but the first one turned out to be a legitimate Windows hearing aid notification icon, so i calmed down. then i found the second one. it was a kaomoji. bleeding. and scattered around it in red letters were B, R, E, E. i rearranged them myself. i know it wasn't telling me about beer. i have a sister named Bree. i still don't know how that file got there. i needed a break after all that so i played some Tomodachi Life. i went to check on my Bree Mii — who i made as accurate to real life as possible, which means she is set to the absolute minimum height. she is tiny. and her voice in the game was cheery and clear but something about it reminded me of the little robot scream from gaymova_ODD.wav. i cannot explain why. it just did. came back from the break to find blood traces near Bree's room. turned out the dog had dragged a squirrel inside. Bree was fine. i went to check her room anyway and looked in her closet. two eyes staring back at me from the shadows. i approached with a gun. a figure stepped out. cloaked. masked. holding a can of Raid. he said, in a very ominous voice: "I need to exterminate the pests." it was the exterminator. i thought nothing of it and moved on. so yeah. that was today. the TurboWarp UI is still darker. the cipher is still unread. the PNG is still unexplained. some mysteries don't get solved i guess.
